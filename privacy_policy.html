Privacy Policy

SunStudio Apps built this application as a free app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

We Value Your Privacy

Your privacy and trust are important to us. This app is designed to function fully on your device, and we do not collect, store, or share any personal data or sensitive information. Everything stays on your phone.

Information Collection and Use

This app does not collect or transmit any personal information, browsing data, contact details, or device identifiers. All QR codes generated, scanned, or saved remain entirely on your device.

Permissions Used

This app requests the following permissions only to enable essential features:

  • Camera Permission – used only for scanning QR codes.
  • Storage / Media Permission – used to save generated QR codes to your gallery and to select an image from your gallery for scanning or embedding a logo.
  • Internet Access (if used) – only for sharing QR codes via other apps; no data is sent to our servers.

No data from these permissions is collected or shared with anyone.

QR Code Handling

When you scan or generate a QR code, all processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload or store any QR-related content anywhere.

Cookies

This app does not use cookies.

Security

Since no personal or sensitive information is collected, there are no data risks associated with this application. All operations remain local to your device.

Children’s Privacy

This app does not collect any data from children or adults. It can be safely used by all age groups.
